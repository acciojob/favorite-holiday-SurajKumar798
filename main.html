Maldives

The Maldives is the smallest country in Asia.
Its land area is only 298 square kilometres (115 sq mi), but this is spread over roughly 90,000 square kilometres (35,000 sq mi) of the sea, making it one of the world's most spatially dispersed sovereign states.
With a population of 515,132 in the 2022 census, it is the second least populous country in Asia and the ninth-smallest country by area, but also one of the most densely populated countries.
The Maldives has an average ground-level elevation of 1.5 metres (4 ft 11 in) above sea level,and a highest natural point of only 2.4 metres (7 ft 10 in), making it the world's lowest-lying country. Some sources state the highest point, Mount Villingili, as 5.1 metres or 17 feet.

Top 5 attractions

  1. Matterhorn - One of the most famous mountains in the world.
  2. Lake Geneva- A stunning lake surrounded by vineyards and mountains.
  3. Jungfraujoch - Known as the "Top of Europe" with breathtaking views.
  4. Château de Chillon - A medieval castle on the shores of Lake Geneva.
  5. Lucerne's Old Town - A charming historic area with beautiful architecture.

Must try foods

  • Swiss Fondue - A delicious melted cheese dish served with bread.
  • Rösti - A crispy Swiss potato dish similar to hash browns.
  • Swiss Chocolate - World-famous, rich, and creamy chocolate.
  • Zürcher Geschnetzeltes - A traditional Swiss veal dish in a creamy sauce.
  • Bircher Muesli - A healthy Swiss breakfast dish made with oats and fruits.
